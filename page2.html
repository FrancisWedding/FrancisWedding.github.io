Aanwysings Na De Hoop

Self-drive from Cape Town (direct route approximately 3 - 3.5hours):
Take N2 to Caledon and onto Riviersonderend. From there travel approximately 45km along the N2 towards Swellendam and turn right onto gravel road, sign posted Spitskop (approx. 13km before Swellendam). Continue on this road for about 32km until you reach a T-junction. Turn left at the T-junction sign posted Malgas / Infanta and travel approximately 2km before you turn right onto the De Hoop Pad signed BUCHU BUSHCAMP / DE HOOP. It is 6km from this turn off to the Main Gate of the Park and a further 5km to the de Hoop Reception.

Self-drive from Bredasdorp 
From Bredasdorp turn left at the Engen Garage and take the R319 to Swellendam out of town for about 6kms and then turn right - sign posted De Hoop / Malgas / Infanta. Travel on the gravel road for 33kms until you see a sign to the right - BUCHU BUSHCAMP / DE HOOP. It is 6km from this turn off to the Main Gate of the Park and a further 5km to the de Hoop Reception.

Self-drive from Swellendam drive on N2 for 13km in the direction of Cape Town, turn left onto gravel road, signed Spitskop. Continue on this road for about 32km until you reach a T-junction. Turn left at the T-junction signed Malgas / Infanta and travel approximately 2km before you turn right onto the De Hoop Pad signed BUCHU BUSHCAMP / DE HOOP. It is 2km from this turn off to the Main Gate of the Park and a further 5km to the de Hoop Reception.
